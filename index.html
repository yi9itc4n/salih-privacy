Privacy Policy

Salih — Islamic Companion App · Last updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

Salih ("the App") is developed by Yiğit Can Hacer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Salih is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal data:

  • Location Data: The App uses your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Location data is processed on your device and sent to the Aladhan API solely to retrieve prayer times. We do not store your location on any server.
  • Usage Data: Prayer logs, streak data, tasbeeh counts, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com): Used to fetch prayer times based on your coordinates. See their privacy policy for details.
  • Google AdMob: Non-premium users may see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for in-app subscription purchases. Transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

Data Storage

All personal data (prayer logs, settings, dhikr counts) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers and do not collect, store, or process your personal data on remote servers.

Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (Monthly Premium and Yearly Premium) to remove advertisements. Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store and are subject to Apple's terms and conditions.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
